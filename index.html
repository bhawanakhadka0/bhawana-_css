Chocolate Ice Cream

Interesting Facts....

Chocolate ice cream is the second most common flavor of ice cream in all over the country.

Chocolate ice cream was first introduced in Naples, Italy in 1692.

It is made by blending cocoa powder with eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar.

In the early 1800s, Thomas Jefferson was one of the first Americans to have a recipe for chocolate ice cream.

Chocolate ice cream is not just a dessert; it is also used in various culinary applications like milkshakes, sundaes, and ice cream cakes.
